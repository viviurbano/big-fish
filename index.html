This is Big Fish

1992

Big Fish was born

Big Fish is a Brazilian funk singer, born in 1992 in Bangu, Rio de Janeiro. He is the youngest son of Dona Maria and Mr. José, among 7 other children.

2006

The start of composing

The singer began composing at age 15, influenced by a turbulent adolescence exposed to violence and precarious life around him.

2007

Youtube channel and parties

Big Fish started out by posting singing videos on YouTube. Eventually, at a party, the emcee said he needed someone to sing. From then on, the party organizers started calling No Name to sing at other events. From the beginning, his musical career was based on the Brazilian musical genre, called funk

2010

Hits are on the air

The singer releases several hits on music platforms such as Spotify and on Brazilian radio stations, such as 'No passinho', 'Bora lá' and 'Os Menino'.
